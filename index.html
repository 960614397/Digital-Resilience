POPIA Changes Everything for South African Businesses

The Protection of Personal Information Act carries fines of up to R10 million and criminal liability for responsible parties. A breach is no longer just an IT problem — it is a board-level legal and financial exposure. Most organisations are still not adequately prepared.

Cost of Inaction

What Happens When You're Not Ready

The cost of a breach far exceeds the cost of prevention. Here is what organisations that delay action typically face.

Operational Shutdown

Business Disruption

Ransomware can take an organisation offline for days or weeks. Every hour of downtime carries a direct revenue cost — plus recovery, forensics, and remediation expenses on top.

Up to R10M in Fines

Regulatory Penalties

POPIA, PCI-DSS, and sector-specific regulations carry significant financial penalties for inadequate data protection. Regulators are increasingly active in enforcement across South Africa.

Permanent Brand Damage

Customer & Reputational Loss

60% of organisations that suffer a significant breach lose major clients within a year. The reputational cost with customers, investors, and partners can outlast any technical recovery.

Third-Party Liability

Supply Chain Exposure

Your security posture is only as strong as your weakest supplier. Attackers increasingly target vendors to access their larger clients — and your organisation may bear the liability for that breach.

Use Cases

The Problems We Solve

Recognise any of these? These are the most common security challenges our clients bring to us — and how we resolve them.

🔐

"We Had a Near-Miss. We Can't Be This Exposed Again."

The Challenge

An organisation relying on legacy AV with no 24/7 monitoring experienced a near-miss ransomware event. Leadership demanded a complete rethink of detection and response — fast.

Our Approach

Deployed ZeroDwell Containment at the endpoint — isolating unknown processes before execution — combined with our 24/7 SOC for real-time investigation and response.

OutcomeZero successful ransomware executions post-deployment. Mean time to detect reduced from days to under 8 minutes.
🏢

"We Have No Visibility Into Our Suppliers' Security."

The Challenge

Hundreds of technology vendors with no systematic way to assess their cyber posture — creating significant regulatory exposure and supply chain risk.

Our Approach

Deployed Third-Party Cyber Risk Intelligence with continuous vendor rating, Ransomware Susceptibility Indexing, and automated alerts on supplier posture changes.

OutcomeFull vendor risk visibility within 30 days. High-risk suppliers identified and remediation mandated before any incident occurred.
📜

"Our Audit Is Coming and We're Not Ready."

The Challenge

An imminent compliance audit with poor data classification, inadequate logging, and no clear process for managing sensitive data — risking failure and regulatory penalty.

Our Approach

DLP controls, SIEM with compliance-specific correlation rules, and vCISO oversight driving a structured programme with automated evidence collection.

OutcomeFull compliance achieved within 6 months. Audit preparation time reduced by 70%.
📋

"We Hold Sensitive Data With No Formal Protection Programme."

The Challenge

Large volumes of personal information, no appointed Information Officer, and unclear data flows — a serious POPIA liability with no remediation plan in place.

Our Approach

Full POPIA gap assessment, data governance framework, DLP implementation, and ongoing vCISO support acting as a virtual Information Officer.

OutcomePOPIA-compliant programme established in 4 months. Breach response and regulator notification procedures formally documented.
🏭

"Our OT Environment Is Growing With Zero Security Visibility."

The Challenge

Expanding IT/OT connectivity with legacy industrial systems — no asset inventory, no patching cadence, and no incident response capability in the operational environment.

Our Approach

Continuous OT asset discovery and vulnerability assessment integrated into our SOC for unified IT/OT threat monitoring and remediation prioritisation.

OutcomeComplete OT asset inventory achieved for the first time. 47 critical vulnerabilities remediated proactively before any incident.
👥

"Our People Keep Clicking the Wrong Things."

The Challenge

Repeated incidents driven by employee behaviour — phishing clicks, data mishandling, and security bypasses — despite mandatory annual training that clearly wasn't working.

Our Approach

Human Risk Management with psychographic profiling targeting highest-risk users with personalised interventions, real-world phishing simulations, and UEBA monitoring.

OutcomePhishing click-through rate reduced by 68% in 90 days. High-risk user cohort halved within 6 months.
